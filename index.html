In the Area

Things in the Area

Grundy Lake Provincial Park

Sandy beaches, hiking trails, and calm lake swimming. One of the best provincial parks in the region.

French River Provincial Park

A Canadian Heritage River with world-class paddling, portaging, and some of the best walleye and bass fishing in Ontario.

Golf

A local golf course nearby for a round between lake days.

Alban

A 10-minute drive to the town of Alban for a diner, ice cream stand, and a fish n' chips stand. Everything you need for a quick outing.

LCBO & Lumieux Store

An LCBO and Lumieux grocery store both about 10 minutes away for drinks, snacks, and essentials.

Noelville Groceries

A full grocery store less than 20 minutes away in Noelville for bigger shops and stocking up.

Location

18 Mile Bay, French River

Getting Here

Take Highway 400 to Highway 69 north. Just over 3 hours from Mississauga, 2 hours from Barrie.

The French River is a Canadian Heritage River, renowned for fishing (walleye, bass, pike, muskie), paddling, and some of the most beautiful sunsets in Ontario.
